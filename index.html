PROJECT: TEXT-iles

In 2023, I started writing a series of software that incorporate my poetry with movements of textile materials, which later became larger projects exploring the relationships between narratives and textiles.

Each of the projects in TEXT-iles started from a snippet of code, and then turned into an interactive web page (linked in the upper row in the section below), then a tangible, interactive installation (lower row).
